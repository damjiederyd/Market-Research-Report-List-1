<p><p>The scleritis market is categorized into four main types based on their characteristics. Diffuse Anterior Scleritis involves widespread inflammation of the anterior sclera, often causing pain and redness. Nodular Anterior Scleritis features localized nodules within the sclera, leading to discomfort and potential vision issues. Necrotizing Anterior Scleritis is a severe form with tissue destruction, requiring aggressive treatment. Posterior Scleritis impacts the back of the eye, causing severe symptoms and complications. Each type demands distinct treatment approaches, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
