<p><p>The puncture-proof tire system market includes two primary types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires feature layers of fabric that cross each other at angles, providing a sturdy build and superior load capacity, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, radial tires consist of fabric layers arranged radially from the center, offering enhanced flexibility, better fuel efficiency, and improved traction. Both types are essential in various vehicles, addressing the need for durability and safety in diverse driving conditions.</p></p>
