<p><strong>The Irrigation Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Submersible</li><li>Non-submersible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The irrigation pumps market is primarily categorized into submersible and non-submersible types. Submersible pumps operate underwater, making them highly efficient for deep well applications and minimizing surface evaporation. They are often used for groundwater extraction and can handle high-pressure requirements. Non-submersible pumps, on the other hand, operate above the water level and are typically used for surface water sources. They are easier to maintain and are suitable for various agricultural applications, including drip and sprinkler irrigation systems.</p></p>
