<p><p>The labyrinthitis market is categorized into two main types: autoimmune labyrinthitis and infective labyrinthitis. Autoimmune labyrinthitis occurs when the body's immune system mistakenly attacks the inner ear structures, leading to inflammation and complications in balance and hearing. In contrast, infective labyrinthitis arises from bacterial, viral, or fungal infections that directly invade the inner ear, causing similar symptoms. Each type requires distinct treatment approaches and poses unique market dynamics, reflecting different patient needs and therapeutic solutions.</p></p>
