<p><ul><li>Pulse Oximeters</li><li>Spirometers</li><li>Capnographs</li><li>Peak flow meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The patient monitors market includes various critical devices that assess and manage patient health. Pulse oximeters measure blood oxygen saturation and pulse rate, vital in respiratory monitoring. Spirometers evaluate lung function, aiding in the diagnosis of pulmonary conditions. Capnographs monitor carbon dioxide levels in exhaled air, essential for assessing ventilation. Peak flow meters measure airflow in asthma patients, providing crucial data for managing respiratory diseases. Together, these devices enhance patient care by ensuring timely and accurate monitoring of respiratory health.</p></p>
