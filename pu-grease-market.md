<p><ul><li>Mineral-Oil Based</li><li>Synthetic-Oil Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PU grease market is characterized by two main types: mineral-oil based and synthetic-oil based greases. Mineral-oil based greases are derived from refined petroleum and offer good performance in moderate conditions, making them cost-effective for various applications. In contrast, synthetic-oil based greases are formulated from chemically engineered compounds, providing superior thermal stability, oxidation resistance, and longer service life. These characteristics make synthetic options ideal for demanding environments, such as automotive and industrial applications, where performance is critical.</p></p>
