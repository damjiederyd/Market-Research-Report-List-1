<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Smoke Alarms</li><li>Ionization Smoke Alarms</li><li>Combination Smoke Alarms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smoke detector market is categorized into three main types: photoelectric smoke alarms, ionization smoke alarms, and combination smoke alarms. Photoelectric alarms use light sensors to detect smoke from smoldering fires, making them effective for slow-burning fires. Ionization alarms utilize radioactive material to sense fast-burning fires, offering quick detection of flaming fires. Combination smoke alarms merge both technologies, providing comprehensive protection against various fire types. Each type serves distinct environments and enhances overall safety in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
