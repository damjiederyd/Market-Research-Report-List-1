<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Neurofeedback Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slow Cortical Potential Neurofeedback (SCP-NF)</li><li>Low-Energy Neurofeedback System (LENS)</li><li>Hemoencephalographic (HEG) Neurofeedback</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Neurofeedback encompasses various market types, each employing distinct methodologies. Slow Cortical Potential Neurofeedback (SCP-NF) focuses on enhancing brain's slow waves to regulate states of attention and mood. Low-Energy Neurofeedback System (LENS) operates on real-time feedback with minimal energy, allowing subtle brainwave adjustments. Hemoencephalographic (HEG) Neurofeedback measures blood flow and oxygen use in the brain, promoting cognitive enhancement. Other neurofeedback modalities include various techniques tailored for specific conditions or objectives, broadening the application landscape within cognitive training and rehabilitation.</p></p>
