<p><p>Cloud encryption gateways are categorized into three market types: public, private, and hybrid clouds. Public cloud encryption gateways offer security for data stored in third-party cloud services, ensuring that sensitive information remains protected. Private cloud gateways, meanwhile, provide encryption for data within a dedicated infrastructure, enhancing control and compliance. Hybrid cloud gateways combine both public and private solutions, allowing organizations to manage and secure their data across diverse environments, balancing flexibility and security in their cloud strategies.</p></p>
