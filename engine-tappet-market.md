<p><strong>The Engine Tappet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Tappet</li><li>Roller Tappet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engine tappet market primarily consists of flat tappets and roller tappets. Flat tappets are traditional designs that rely on a flat surface to follow the camshaft profile, providing reliability but potentially generating more friction and wear. Roller tappets, on the other hand, utilize a cylindrical roller to reduce friction, offering improved performance and efficiency. As engine technology advances, the roller tappet segment is gaining popularity due to its enhanced durability and ability to support higher engine speeds compared to flat tappets.</p></p>
