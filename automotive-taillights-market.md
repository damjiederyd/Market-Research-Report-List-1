<p><p>The automotive taillights market comprises several types of lighting technologies. Incandescent lamps are traditional, providing soft light but are less energy-efficient and have shorter lifespans. Light Emitting Diodes (LEDs) offer increased brightness, longevity, and energy efficiency, making them the most popular choice. High Intensity Discharge (HID) lights provide intense illumination and are commonly used in luxury vehicles. Neon tubes, while less common, offer vibrant aesthetics and are mainly used for decorative purposes or custom installations in vehicles.</p></p>
