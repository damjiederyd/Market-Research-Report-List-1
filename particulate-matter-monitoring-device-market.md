<p><strong>The Particulate Matter Monitoring Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Light Scattering</li><li>Beta-attenuation</li><li>Gravimetric</li><li>Opacity</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The particulate matter monitoring device market encompasses several types, each employing different technologies. Light scattering measures particle concentration by assessing light scattered by particles. Beta-attenuation quantifies particulate mass by measuring the reduction of beta radiation passing through a sample. Gravimetric methods involve collecting particles on filters and weighing them to determine mass. Opacity devices assess how much light is blocked by particles in the air. Other types may include advanced techniques like optical and chemical sensors, broaden the monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
