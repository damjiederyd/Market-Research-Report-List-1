<p><p>The Respiratory Gas Humidifier market is categorized into two primary types: active and passive humidifiers. Active humidifiers use a power source to actively heat or add moisture to the air, enhancing respiratory therapy efficiency. They are often used in hospital settings for critically ill patients. In contrast, passive humidifiers rely on ambient heat and moisture from exhaled air, making them simpler and more cost-effective for home use. Both types aim to improve patient comfort and respiratory function during treatment.</p></p>
