<p><p>The silver ion selective electrodes market is primarily divided into two types: crystal membrane and PVC membrane electrodes. Crystal membrane electrodes utilize a solid-state membrane made from crystal materials, providing high stability and selectivity for silver ions, making them suitable for precise measurements in various applications. In contrast, PVC membrane electrodes employ a polymeric matrix that incorporates ion-selective materials, offering flexibility and ease of use. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including environmental monitoring, pharmaceuticals, and water quality testing.</p></p>
