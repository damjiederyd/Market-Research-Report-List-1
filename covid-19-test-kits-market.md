<p><p>The COVID-19 test kits market comprises several types, including Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs), Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISAs), and Neutralization Assays. RDTs provide quick results using minimal equipment, ideal for remote settings. ELISAs are laboratory-based tests that measure antibodies or antigens, offering greater sensitivity and specificity. Neutralization Assays assess the ability of antibodies to inhibit virus activity, providing insights into immune response. These diverse testing methods cater to different needs in diagnosing and managing COVID-19 infections.</p></p>
