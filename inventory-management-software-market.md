<p><p>Inventory management software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on the vendor's servers and accessible via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and reduced upfront costs. It facilitates real-time updates and remote access. In contrast, on-premise software is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control and customization but requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different business needs and operational preferences in managing inventory effectively.</p></p>
