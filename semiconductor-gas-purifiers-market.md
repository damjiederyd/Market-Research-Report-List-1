<p><ul><li>Point of Use Gas Purifiers</li><li>Bulk Gas Purifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor gas purifiers market includes two main types: Point of Use Gas Purifiers and Bulk Gas Purifiers. Point of Use Gas Purifiers are designed for specific applications, delivering high-purity gases directly to semiconductor manufacturing processes, ensuring contamination-free environments. In contrast, Bulk Gas Purifiers handle larger volumes of gases, often used for centralized gas distribution systems, providing cost-effective solutions for high-demand manufacturing facilities. Both types are essential for maintaining the purity and performance of gases used in semiconductor production.</p></p>
