<p><p>The hypertension drugs market comprises several key types. Calcium channel blockers relax blood vessels by blocking calcium entry into cells. Diuretics promote fluid excretion to lower blood pressure. Beta blockers reduce heart rate and cardiac output. Vasodilators directly relax blood vessels. Angiotensin converting enzyme inhibitors prevent the formation of a hormone that narrows blood vessels. Alpha blockers relax blood vessels by blocking nerve signals. Angiotensin receptor blockers inhibit hormone effects, while renin inhibitors reduce renin production to manage hypertension.</p></p>
