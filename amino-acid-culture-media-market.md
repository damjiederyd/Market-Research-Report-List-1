<p><p>The amino acid culture media market is categorized into two main types: basic medium and serum-free medium. Basic medium is designed to support cell growth using essential amino acids, vitamins, and salts, providing a foundational environment for various cell types. In contrast, serum-free medium is formulated to eliminate the need for animal serum, utilizing synthetic or chemically defined components to reduce variability and enhance consistency in cell culture. Both types are crucial in biotechnology and pharmaceutical developments for efficient cell cultivation.</p></p>
