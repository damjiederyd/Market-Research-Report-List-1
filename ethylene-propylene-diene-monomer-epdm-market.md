<p><p>Ethylene-Propylene-Diene Monomer (EPDM) is a versatile synthetic rubber used in various applications, including automotive, construction, and electronics. The market for EPDM can be segmented by production methods: solution polymerization and suspension polymerization. Solution polymerization, favored for its ability to produce high-quality rubber with specific properties, involves dissolving the monomers in a solvent. In contrast, suspension polymerization occurs within a liquid medium, resulting in larger particle sizes and varied rubber attributes, catering to different industrial requirements and applications.</p></p>
