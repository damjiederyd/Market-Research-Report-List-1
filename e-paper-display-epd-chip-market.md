<p><p>The E-paper Display (EPD) chip market is categorized into two main types: Discrete Controllers and System on Chip (SoC). Discrete Controllers are dedicated chips designed specifically to manage the EPD, offering flexibility and compatibility with various display sizes. In contrast, SoC integrates multiple functions, including display control and processing, into a single chip, providing a compact solution with reduced power consumption. Both types cater to evolving applications in digital signage, e-readers, and smart devices, enhancing user experience through energy-efficient displays.</p></p>
