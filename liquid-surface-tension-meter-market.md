<p><strong>The Liquid Surface Tension Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Liquid Surface Tension Meter</li><li>Dynamic Liquid Surface Tension Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid surface tension meter market consists of two main types: static and dynamic meters. Static liquid surface tension meters measure the force required to detach a probe or liquid drop from a surface at equilibrium, providing precise readings suitable for various applications. Dynamic liquid surface tension meters, on the other hand, assess the tension by observing liquid behavior during motion or agitation, offering insights into the surface tension changes over time. Both types are crucial for industries like cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and food.</p></p>
