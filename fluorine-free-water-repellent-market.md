<p><p>The Fluorine-free Water Repellent market comprises three main types: **Weak Cationic**, **Weakly Anionic**, and **Non-ionic**. Weak Cationic water repellents possess a slight positive charge, enhancing adhesion to negatively charged surfaces, such as textiles. Weakly Anionic products carry a mild negative charge, providing effective water repellency while ensuring compatibility with various substrates. Non-ionic water repellents are neutral, offering versatility in application and stability across different pH levels, making them suitable for a wide range of uses while maintaining eco-friendly properties.</p></p>
