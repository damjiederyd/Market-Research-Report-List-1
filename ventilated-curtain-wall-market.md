<p><p>The ventilated curtain wall market is primarily categorized into two types: unitised and stick-built systems. Unitised systems consist of pre-assembled panels that are manufactured off-site and installed as single units, offering faster installation and reduced labor costs. In contrast, stick-built systems are constructed on-site, where individual components are assembled to form the wall. While stick-built allows for greater customization and flexibility, unitised systems provide efficiency and quality control, appealing to different project requirements in modern architectural applications.</p></p>
