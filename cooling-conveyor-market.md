<p><p>The cooling conveyor market features various types tailored for specific cooling needs. Spiral cooling conveyors efficiently utilize vertical space, enabling continuous product cooling in a compact design. Three-way cooling conveyors facilitate effective cooling by allowing products to be diverted in multiple directions, enhancing workflow. Air cooling conveyors use ambient or chilled air to lower product temperatures as they move, making them ideal for lightweight items. Each type serves distinct industries, balancing space efficiency, cooling speed, and product handling.</p></p>
