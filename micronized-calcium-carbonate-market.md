<p><ul><li>Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC)</li><li>Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micromized calcium carbonate market is divided into two main types: Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC) and Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC). GCC is produced by finely grinding natural limestone or marble, yielding larger particle sizes suitable for various applications, including construction and plastics. In contrast, PCC is manufactured through a chemical process, resulting in smaller, purer particles with enhanced performance qualities. Both types serve diverse industries, including paper, paint, rubber, and food, offering unique properties tailored to specific applications.</p></p>
