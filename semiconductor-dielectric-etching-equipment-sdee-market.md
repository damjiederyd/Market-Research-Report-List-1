<p><p>The Semiconductor Dielectric Etching Equipment (SDEE) market is divided into two main types: wet etching equipment and dry etching equipment. Wet etching utilizes liquid chemicals to remove material from semiconductor surfaces, offering high selectivity and uniformity. In contrast, dry etching employs gas-phase chemicals in processes like reactive ion etching (RIE) or plasma etching, enabling precise control over etch profiles and features at nanoscale levels. Both types are crucial for achieving the intricate patterns required in modern semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
