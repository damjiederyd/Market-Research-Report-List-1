<p><strong>The Continuous Glucose Monitoring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wearable</li><li>Implantable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) market is primarily segmented into two types: wearable and implantable devices. Wearable CGMs are external sensors attached to the skin, providing real-time glucose data without invasive procedures. They are user-friendly and suitable for daily monitoring. Implantable CGMs, on the other hand, involve devices surgically placed under the skin for long-term glucose tracking. These offer more precise readings and extended wear time, catering to individuals with chronic conditions who require consistent monitoring and management of blood glucose levels.</p></p>
