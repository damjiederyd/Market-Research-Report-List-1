<p><ul><li>Potassium Hydroxide Type</li><li>Hydrofluoric Acid/Acetic Acid Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon etchant market comprises two primary types: Potassium Hydroxide (KOH) type and Hydrofluoric Acid/Acetic Acid type. The KOH type is known for its effectiveness in anisotropic etching, primarily used in MEMS and semiconductor applications, while the Hydrofluoric Acid/Acetic Acid combination serves to achieve isotropic etching, often utilized in glass and silicon wafer processing. Both types play a crucial role in semiconductor fabrication, enabling the precise patterning and structuring essential for various electronic devices.</p></p>
